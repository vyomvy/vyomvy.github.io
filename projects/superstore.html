Pipeline 05 / Superstore

Superstore sales analysis

A city-level revenue vs. profit breakdown built to separate cities that look good from cities that actually make money.

Live analysis ↗
The problem

Revenue alone doesn't tell you whether a market is actually worth serving. This analysis set out to find the gap between "sales look good" and "the business is actually making money" at a city level.

The approach
01
Clean & structure with Pandas

Prepared the Superstore dataset for city-level aggregation and comparison.

02
Compare revenue vs. profit

Ranked cities on both metrics side by side instead of revenue alone.

Key findings
High sales

Several cities with strong revenue were quietly loss-making once profit was checked.

Real engines

A distinct set of cities emerged as consistently strong profit drivers — the ones worth investing in.
